Overview

Leading PR for Austin's student indie scene

Groovy Goat was a student-run music festival celebrating Austin's indie music culture, produced entirely by St. Edward's University students. As PR & Marketing Lead, I owned the full publicity strategy — from initial awareness campaigns through event day coverage.

The role required managing a small PR team, coordinating across departments, and executing multi-channel campaigns that built genuine buzz on campus and in the surrounding community.

Key Contributions

What I Did

  • Led the full publicity campaign strategy for the festival, setting goals, timelines, and messaging across all channels.
  • Coordinated multi-channel campaigns spanning social media, campus flyers, pop-up tabling events, and cross-departmental email outreach.
  • Managed a PR sub-team — delegating tasks, building shared calendars, and maintaining a collaborative creative environment under deadline pressure.
  • Developed and executed pop-up promotional events on campus to drive ticket awareness and engagement.
  • Fostered relationships with student organizations, faculty, and external vendors to amplify reach beyond the core student body.
