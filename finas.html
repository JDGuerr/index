Overview

Growing a local brand through storytelling

Fina's Cooking & Catering is a family-run catering business based in Laredo, TX. Starting as a Digital Marketing Intern in August 2025 and promoted to Part-Time Social Media Manager in January 2026, I built and manage the brand's full digital presence from the ground up.

The work centers on translating the warmth and quality of a family business into content that resonates — using storytelling-driven posts, consistent visual branding via Canva, and data-informed decisions to grow the audience and drive real business inquiries.

Key Contributions

What I Did

  • Ran a full social media audit and competitive analysis — tracking competing caterers' services, menus, follower counts, and content strategies, then translating findings into a marketing action plan.
  • Rebuilt the marketing plan from scratch into a single structured document with clear objectives, timelines, success criteria, color-coded rows, and a data-entry guide so the owner could maintain it independently.
  • Redesigned the brand's visual identity — iterated through retro and coffee-shop aesthetic directions in Canva until landing on a style described as "classic but fresh," then worked with a web designer to apply the new logo and color palette to the website.
  • Helped justify and coordinate a website hosting upgrade to improve stability, weighing cost against long-term reliability for the owner's business.
  • Created QR codes for packaging (meal labels and thank-you cards) with UTM tracking parameters to drive opt-in list growth and referrals.
  • Drafted outreach emails for B2B prospecting and customer acquisition, combining messaging strategy with an awareness of the brand's community positioning.
  • Managed the brand's Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp presence — publishing storytelling-driven content, designing posts and stories in Canva, and building a content calendar to ensure consistent publishing.
  • Catered for the City of Laredo mayoral offices — a recurring high-profile client whose continued business validates the brand's service quality.
