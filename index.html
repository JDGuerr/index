Chapter One — It's You, Lyla

Zaelar 3, 1085 PM; The Third Age — Cassan Province

All was quiet, all except for the gentle winds and waves crashing beneath the rock-infested sea beyond. It was all Lyla could hear. The morning chill clung to the bodies inside the courtyard, biting at the fingertips of the gathered prospects.

She raised her sword — a blade with ornate filigree along the hilt. Her mother's blade. Her knuckles whitened as she gripped the handle tighter. The man she faced readied his blade. She scanned him closely, igniting a spark in her eyes.

"BEGIN!" A voice in the far-off side of her vision shouted.

Her eyes darted quickly from one side to another. She closed both eyes, shutting them tight. Taking a long breath, poised and ready. Lyla lunged forward, thrusting her blade into her opponent. He parried her attempt, knocking the sword back to her. She staggered.

"Timing, Prospect," her opponent sang — "Timing is everything."
